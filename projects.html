Time perception calculator
  • As you grow up, time seems to pass faster
  • One of the reasons is that you are having less new experiences
  • Another reason is that a time period like 1 month is a smaller fraction of your life now than when you were 5 years old.
  • This program calculates what time period we feel now is the same as a set time period we felt at a set age.
  • For example "As a 7 years old, 3 months feels as long as 6.857142857142857 months feels for me as a 16 year old"
